About us

At Cohere we’re on a mission to unleash the potential of refugees so they can determine their own futures on their own terms.

WE ARE COHERE

At COHERE, we believe that everyone's future should be in their own hands, and we are dedicated to making this vision a reality. Our dedicated team partners with refugee leaders and organisations across the African continent to support them with the tools and resources they need to build stronger, self-sustaining communities, for today and the future.
