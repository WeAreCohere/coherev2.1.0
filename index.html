WHAT WE DO

Cohere supports the most marginalised by...

Following the Lead of Refugee Communities

Acting as an ally to those most affected, listening to their needs, and supporting them as they adapt to ever-changing realities.

Partnering with Refugee Leaders

Building trusted relationships with those who know their communities best, ensuring responses are locally driven and inclusive.

Crisis Response

Building communities of people committed to supporting refugee-led organisations as they navigate crises and adapt to challenges.

SUPPORT SUDAN

Donate to refugee-led organisations on the front lines, providing urgent support to displaced communities affected by the war. Your contribution helps deliver critical provisions, shelter, healthcare, and hope. Together, we can rebuild lives. Get involved today.

UNITY

Unity is at the heart of what we do at Cohere. By coming together, we can create a world where every voice is heard, and refugee communities across the African continent have the resources they need to transform their futures. Get involved today!

ARE WE ALL LISTENING?

With displacement rising, effective responses start by listening—to the expertise, experiences, and ideas of refugee leaders shaping solutions for their communities. Listening deepens our understanding, expands knowledge, and strengthens humanity. Together, we can amplify their voices and create lasting change.
