Join our community

In an increasingly divided and isolating world, the power of unity has never been more vital. At Cohere, we believe that real change happens when we come together—refugees, displaced communities, donors, activists, leaders, and everyone who believes in creating a better, fairer world.

Why Join the Community?

Amplify Impact

Unite with people from all walks of life who are committed to making a difference for displaced communities across Africa.

Champion Inclusion

Be part of a collective effort to ensure that the most vulnerable voices are heard, valued, and empowered.

Build Connections

Engage with like-minded individuals who share your passion for human rights and your belief in the power of community-led solutions.

Take Action

Together, we can challenge the status quo, reimagine humanitarian systems, and create meaningful, lasting change.

Your Voice Matters

Joining our community is about more than supporting a cause—it’s about being part of something bigger than yourself. It’s about standing alongside displaced communities, listening to their needs, and working together to create a world where everyone can thrive.

Stay Connected

Sign up to receive updates, stories, and opportunities to engage.

Collaborate

Share your skills, knowledge, or resources to support our mission.

Spread the Word

Help amplify the call for unity by sharing our vision with your network.

As global crises increase and more people are forced to leave their homes, coming together has never been more urgent—or more powerful.
