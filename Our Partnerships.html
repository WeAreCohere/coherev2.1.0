We collaborate with 73+ refugee-led and community-based organizations across Africa, providing funding, capacity-building, coordination, and advocacy support.

Trust-Based Partnerships

At Cohere, we believe in fostering strong, trust-based relationships. By listening, understanding, and sharing, we empower our partners to lead their own solutions.

The Trust Circle Model

The Trust Circle Model ensures an equitable, transparent approach to partnerships. It removes biases, enhances knowledge-sharing, and strengthens relationships with refugee-led organizations.

Collaborative Decision-Making

Each Trust Circle consists of Cohere team members working closely with RLOs. Through co-design and regular feedback, we maximize impact and ensure continuous improvement.
